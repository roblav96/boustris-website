Company Summary

We are a family owned company that has been established for 24 years with combined 40 years experience. We employ 25 qualified electrical technicians dispatching 17 service trucks daily in the greater Boston area. We specialize in fire alarm & sprinkler testing, inspection, troubleshooting, monitoring, and offer many more services. We only service property management companies so we understand the importance in prompt quality service.
